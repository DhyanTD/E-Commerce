The ‘Online E-commerce Web application’ Services department strives to provide solutions to develop and transfer easy and efficient way in the digital age and to help reduces the human pressure and time. To help support shop collections, the digital initiatives, and external partner institution digital projects, It provide services that include the digitization of analog objects, metadata management, digital preservation, and discovery and access of digital collections. “Shop Management System” is a web application written for all operating systems, designed to help users maintain and organize shop virtually. This software is easy to use for both beginners and advanced users. It features a familiar and well thought-out, an attractive user interface, combined with strong searching Insertion and reporting capabilities. The report generation facility of shop system helps to get a good idea of which are the various items brought by the members, makes users possible to get the product easily.
